Who Am I And What Do I Do?

Many people seek out life coaches for guidance in navigating a significant life change, such as taking on a new career. In plenty of cases, however, people turn to life coaches simply for help in building a happier, more meaningful life. There are a number of indications that working with a life coach could be helpful for you. These signs include: Frequent irritability High levels of stress and/or anxiety Inability to break bad habits Lack of fulfillment in your social life Persistent feeling of dissatisfaction at work Sense of blocked creativity
